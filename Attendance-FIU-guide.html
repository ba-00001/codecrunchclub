Code Crunch Meeting attendance Guide

FIU students

Steps for new and current members to prepare for our club meetings.

LinkedIn Instagram WhatsApp Discord Panther Connect Linktree

Steps for New Members:

  • Become an official member by:
    • Filling out the membership form on the club website.
    • Completing your registration on Panther Connect
    • Joining the Discord for club communications. (You will receive the link to join after you complete the membership form).
    • Following us on LinkedIn and Instagram for updates.
  • Record your attendance (after you complete the membership form).

Steps for Current Members:

  • Record your attendance (including session attendance).

RSVP Before Each Club Meeting:

  • RSVP for the meeting (for special events, this helps us estimate attendance for giveaways, food, etc. Don't forget!). You can RSVP in advance for weekly meetings.
  • You can RSVP for all meetings available in advance on Luma.
