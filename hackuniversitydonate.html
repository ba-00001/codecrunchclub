Invest in Student Innovation Through Year-Round Hackathons

Hack University runs monthly hackathons and mini-hacks that give students practical experience in AI, cloud, cybersecurity, DevOps, blockchain, XR, and product strategy. Your support directly funds mentorship, project resources, and equitable access for students across majors and backgrounds.

Program Structure

Each monthly event includes two tracks so both technical and non-technical students can participate and deliver meaningful outcomes.

Tech Challenge (Build Track)

Participants design and build working software or hardware solutions. Example: an AI-powered study group matching platform.

Technopreneurship Challenge (No-Code Track)

Participants build an evidence-based business pitch and implementation roadmap. Example: a campus logistics startup concept.

Standard Event Format

We combine long-form project development with focused execution windows to maximize both learning and shipping outcomes.

Session Timing Primary Outcome
Week-Long Online Build Sprint Friday to Friday (about 168 hours) Team formation, workshops, mentor check-ins, solution architecture
Mini-Hack Build Sprint Saturday 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM (12 hours) Rapid build, final delivery, and judging in a concentrated format
