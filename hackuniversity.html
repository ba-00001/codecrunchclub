Our Mission

Founded in August 2024, Hack University unites multiple student organizations and global communities to empower the next generation of innovators through workshops, hackathons, and mentorship across disciplines and borders.

Membership Benefits

  • • Access to 20 annual interdisciplinary hackathons
  • • Interview prep, resume reviews, and coaching
  • • Startup pitch development & entrepreneurship mentoring
  • • Executive Board application opportunities
  • • Cross-discipline networking and industry access

Community Access

  • Discord: coding help, team formation, mentorship (secured after OneStop)
  • GroupMe: quick coordination & announcements (Open to all)
  • Instagram Group highlights & networking (Open to all)
