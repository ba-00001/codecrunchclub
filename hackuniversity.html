Technical Competition
Are you a CS or engineering major?

305 Tech Build Hackathon (Code or Hardware)

This competition is built for students who want to design, code, prototype, and ship technical solutions using software, hardware, or both together.

Why this matters for your career: You build a real project portfolio, strengthen technical interview readiness, and gain hands-on experience that helps with internships and full-time engineering roles.

Start-Up Competition
Are you a business major or entrepreneur student?

305 Code to Cash: Develop the Next Business Startup Idea

This competition focuses on turning technical ideas into startup-ready concepts, market opportunities, and business pitches.

Why this matters for your career: You practice product thinking, communication, and go-to-market strategy, which are key for founder paths, consulting roles, product management, and business leadership tracks.

Analytics Competition
Are you a student interested in data analytics or data science?

305 Business Data Analytics / Data Science

This competition is centered on data storytelling, analytics, dashboards, spreadsheet modeling, and decision-making under time pressure.

Why this matters for your career: You develop evidence-based problem solving, data communication, and dashboard skills used in analytics, finance, operations, and data science careers.

Our Mission

Founded in August 2024, Hack University unites multiple student organizations and global communities to empower the next generation of innovators through workshops, hackathons, and mentorship across disciplines and borders.

Membership Benefits

  • 100% Free with OneStop Membership Form (13+)
  • Access to 20 annual interdisciplinary hackathons
  • Interview prep, resume reviews, and coaching
  • Startup pitch development and entrepreneurship mentoring
  • Executive Board application opportunities
  • Cross-discipline networking and industry access

Community Access

  • Discord: coding help, team formation, mentorship (secured after OneStop)
  • GroupMe: quick coordination & announcements (Open to all)
  • Instagram Group highlights & networking (Open to all)
