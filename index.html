Mission & Purpose ✨

Our Mission

Code Crunch is an interdisciplinary club connecting students across all majors, high schools (16+), and universities through technology and innovation. We empower students through coding workshops, technical interview prep, and project-based learning.

Connecting All Disciplines & Education Levels

We welcome students from diverse academic backgrounds and education levels including:

  • High School Students (16+)
  • Computer Science
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Business
  • College/University Students
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Health Sciences
  • Law
  • And many more!

At Code Crunch, we believe that the most innovative solutions come from collaboration across disciplines.

Join Our Club ✨

Becoming a member of Code Crunch connects you with a diverse community of peers, mentors, and resources to help you succeed in technology and innovation. Open to high school students (16+), all majors, and any university.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to all club events and workshops
  • Mentorship from experienced students and professionals
  • Networking with industry professionals and peers
  • Leadership and volunteer opportunities
  • Hackathon participation and team building

How to Join

Membership is open to all students interested in technology and innovation, including high school students (16+), college students from any major, and from any university worldwide.

To become a member, simply fill out the membership form and join our community events. Free Membership.

Questions about membership? Message us on LinkedIn or DM us on Discord @codecrunchclub
