Welcome to Code Crunch

Code Crunch is bridging global innovation with local engagement, offering a dynamic platform open to all university students, regardless of major—no prior experience required! Freshmen, graduates, and alumni from any university are welcome. Join us to break down barriers in technology, participate in hands-on coding workshops, collaborate on community-driven projects, and prepare for the future through immersive virtual and on-campus experiences.

Whether you’re joining us via our virtual Zoom sessions from anywhere in the world or in person right here at Florida International University, we provide the resources, mentorship, and community you need to grow as a coder and innovator.

Mission

Code Crunch is dedicated to empowering university students by providing hands-on experience in software development, interdisciplinary collaboration, and real-world project building. Through workshops, coding challenges, and peer mentorship, we equip members with the skills needed for technical interviews, industry-relevant problem-solving, and cutting-edge technology applications. Our mission extends beyond traditional coding—we strive to bridge the gap between theory and practice, ensuring that students, regardless of major or prior experience, have access to the tools, resources, and community needed to innovate, create, and succeed in the tech industry.

Vision

Our vision is to foster a global community of innovators and problem-solvers by providing interdisciplinary learning opportunities that integrate software development, engineering, data science, and emerging technologies. We aim to empower students through hands-on projects, industry mentorship, and technical workshops while preparing them for careers in tech, research, and entrepreneurship. By creating an inclusive, collaborative, and knowledge-sharing ecosystem, we ensure that students from all backgrounds, universities, and experience levels can thrive in technology, innovation, and beyond. Through mentorship, alumni engagement, and industry partnerships, we are shaping the next generation of leaders, builders, and changemakers in the digital world.

Let’s innovate together! Built and led by students, for students.

Each Code Crunch course has its own GitHub repository. Weekly files, including Google Slides, code blocks, and other resources, are uploaded to ensure members stay up to date.

Note: Access to all Google Docs and Slides will be granted using the Google account provided in the "Become a Member" form. Ensure the correct account is used during registration.
