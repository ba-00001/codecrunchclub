C4 | Crunch LABS: Hackathon Edition & Team Sign-Up

The C4 course is designed as a 45-minute project development session, followed by 15 to 20 minutes of troubleshooting and planning with Code Crunch's C3 and C4 Tech Leads. During this time, club members can refine their hackathon projects and prepare for success.

Participating in C4 hackathon workshops is open to everyone and does not require team sign-up. Feel free to join the workshops to learn, collaborate, and innovate!

For the C4 Code Crunch program in Spring 2025, the featured project is an instant messaging app built using Java, React, and Spring Boot.

Members who sign up for C4 Teams representing Code Crunch will receive additional support during the last 15 to 20 minutes of each session to troubleshoot their app development for the virtual MHL Hackathons.
