Mission & Purpose ✨

Our Mission

Code Crunch is an interdisciplinary club connecting students across all majors through technology and innovation. We empower students through coding workshops, technical interview prep, and project-based learning.

Connecting All Disciplines

We welcome students from diverse academic backgrounds including:

  • Computer Science
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Business
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Health Sciences
  • Law
  • And many more!

At Code Crunch, we believe that the most innovative solutions come from collaboration across disciplines.
