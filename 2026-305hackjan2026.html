305 Hackathon (3-in-1 Event) ‚Äî January 2026 Edition

Theme: AI in Healthcare & Biotech ¬∑ Hybrid worldwide event

A hackathon is a fast-paced, hands-on problem-solving competition where you turn ideas into real solutions. Hack University hackathons bring students from all majors together to build, pitch, and compete across three different challenge tracks.

A hybrid global 3-in-1 competition that brings together technical innovation, entrepreneurship, and data analytics, all focused on AI-powered solutions in healthcare, biotech, and patient outcomes.

Tech Build Hackathon (Code or Hardware)

Participants design and build technical solutions using software, hardware, or a combination of both.

Why it matters: Builds real-world engineering skills, hands-on problem solving, and project experience students can showcase in portfolios and technical interviews.

Business Venture Arena (Tech-to-Business Pitch)

Teams translate technical ideas into viable, impact-driven business or startup concepts.

Why it matters: Helps students communicate technical ideas, evaluate feasibility, and think beyond code toward real-world adoption and impact.

305 Excel Data Duel (Live Data Analytics Challenge)

A live analytics competition where participants solve timed Excel-based data challenges, earn points, and compete for a top score.

Why it matters: Strengthens analytical thinking, data literacy, and decision-making skills that are essential across business, engineering, and data-driven careers.

Open to everyone worldwide ‚Äî beginners, advanced builders, and non-tech majors welcome. No prior experience needed. Ages 16+ accepted (under 18 requires parental or guardian consent).

Track Competition Overview

Tracks & Judging ‚Äî Shared Across Both Sessions

Criteria: Technical Difficulty ¬∑ Functionality ¬∑ Innovation & Creativity ¬∑ Design & User Experience ¬∑ Impact & Usefulness ¬∑ Presentation & Demo

Track Competition 1 ‚Äî Tech Build (Hackathon)

Code or hardware project. Build a healthcare or biotech solution using AI or data to improve workflows, diagnostics, patient experience, research, or operations. Think dashboards, AI agents, automation tools, or prototypes that can realistically be piloted within 3‚Äì12 months.

Track Competition 2 ‚Äî Business Venture Arena (No-Code)

Shark Tank‚Äìstyle pitch. Design a no-code startup, program, or business model using AI and data in healthcare & biotech. Clearly define your customer, validate the problem, and present a go-to-market plan, sustainability model, and impact strategy.

Track Competition 3 ‚Äî 305 Excel Data Duel (Live Challenge Track)

A live, esports-style Excel competition where participants solve real-world data challenges in timed rounds. Challenges focus on data cleaning, formulas, logic, analysis, and interpretation. Participants earn points based on accuracy and completion, with a single top-scoring winner determined at the end.

The Live Challenge Track takes place during Session B (In-Person). Participation details, challenge access, and rules will be released during the event and listed on the official agenda.

Open to beginners and advanced participants. No prior Excel or data analytics experience is required. Challenges scale in difficulty so all participants can compete, learn, and succeed.

Full prompts for each track unlock only after the opening ceremony for each session. The Live Challenge Track uses a points-based scoring system and is judged separately.
