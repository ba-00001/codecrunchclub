Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about 305 Hackathon Edition: Spring 2026

The 305 Hackathon Edition is a 3-day hybrid event celebrating Miami's tech community (305 area code). It focuses on Tech for Good, AI, and Blockchain solutions that create positive social impact. Join us in-person in Miami or virtually from anywhere!
All students are welcome! University students, high school students (16+), and participants from all majors can join. Whether you're local to the 305 or joining virtually, we celebrate Miami's diverse and inclusive tech community.
No prior experience required! We'll provide comprehensive resources, tutorials, and mentorship focused on AI and blockchain for social good. Many successful projects come from teams learning these technologies during the event.
You can participate in-person in Miami for the full 305 experience or join virtually from anywhere. Both in-person and virtual participants have access to the same resources, mentors, and opportunities to win prizes.

Teams can include both in-person and virtual members! We provide Discord channels, video conferencing tools, and collaboration platforms to ensure seamless teamwork between on-site Miami participants and remote participants.
