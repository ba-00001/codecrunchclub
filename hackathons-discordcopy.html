About the Initiative

Founded in August 2024, the Hack University Initiative is a collaborative effort uniting multiple student organizations and global communities with a mission to empower the next generation of innovators, developers, and leaders. Through workshops, hackathons, and mentorship, Hack University drives innovation and collaboration across disciplines and borders.
