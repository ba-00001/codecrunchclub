Project Inspiration

If you're looking for ideas, here are a few problem statements to consider for your hackathon project:

  • AI for Accessibility: Develop an AI-powered assistant for visually impaired users.
  • Blockchain for Transparency: Build a secure blockchain-based voting system.
  • AI-Powered Debugging: Create a tool that analyzes and fixes errors in code automatically.
  • Sustainable Tech: Use AI to reduce energy consumption in smart homes.

Why Join Code Crunch Hackathons?

  • Open to students of all backgrounds and skill levels.
  • Hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies.
  • Network with tech enthusiasts, mentors, and recruiters.
  • Win prizes and gain industry recognition.
  • Showcase your skills in AI, Blockchain, and Web Development.

We look forward to seeing the innovative projects you will create. Let's build the future together at Code Crunch Hackathons!
