💼 Track 2: Tech Unicorn Challenge

Startup Pitch Competition – Present your innovative startup idea just like raising seed funding

🚀 No coding required! Focus on your vision and business strategy

🏆 Winner

Best Pitch

Winner receives certificate and recognition

📋 What to Submit:

1. Pitch Deck or Interactive Prototype
• Professional pitch deck (10-15 slides) in PowerPoint, Google Slides, or PDF format
• May include: business model canvas, market analysis, financial projections, mockups
• Must be uploaded to your GitHub repository with a working preview link

2. Executive Summary
• Brief description of your startup vision and value proposition
• No more than one page in length (single-spaced, Word/PDF format)
• Explain what makes your idea innovative and market-ready
• Must be uploaded to GitHub repository

3. Pitch Video Presentation
• Present your startup pitch just like you're raising seed funding
• Maximum length: 10 minutes or less
• Upload video to GitHub repository (or provide YouTube/Loom link in README)
• URL must have authorization for judges to view
• Teams may decide which member(s) will present in the video

⚠️ Important: All materials (pitch deck, executive summary, video file or link, and any supporting documents) MUST be uploaded to your GitHub repository. NO CODE is required or allowed for this challenge.

💡 Recommended Tools: PowerPoint, Google Slides, Canva, Figma, Pitch.com, Notion, Loom (for video), or any no-code/low-code platform

📁 GitHub Repository Structure Example:
• README.md (project overview & links)
• pitch-deck.pptx or pitch-deck.pdf
• executive-summary.pdf
• pitch-video.mp4 (or link in README)
• /supporting-docs/ (any additional materials)

Full challenge details unlock at 11:00 PM EST on Oct 13

🎉 Challenge Now Active!

🎯 Challenge Focus Areas:
  • Women in Tech: Solutions that empower women entrepreneurs and close gender gaps in startup funding
  • Ethical AI: AI-powered startups focused on fairness, transparency, and social good
  • Social Impact: Technologies addressing community needs and creating positive change
  • Inclusive Innovation: Accessible products/services for diverse and underserved markets
⚖️ Judging Criteria:
  • Technical Difficulty: Complexity and technical sophistication of the proposed solution
  • Functionality: How well the startup concept solves the identified problem
  • Innovation & Creativity: Originality and uniqueness of the business idea
  • Design & User Experience: Quality of pitch deck design and presentation clarity
  • Impact & Usefulness: Potential positive impact on target market and society
  • Presentation & Demo: Quality and persuasiveness of video pitch delivery
💡 Pitch Tips:
  • Start with a compelling problem statement that resonates with judges
  • Clearly articulate your unique value proposition and competitive advantage
  • Include realistic financial projections and funding requirements
  • Demonstrate deep understanding of your target market and customers
  • Show passion, confidence, and preparedness in your video presentation
  • Address potential risks and your mitigation strategies

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EmpowHER Hackathon 2025

EmpowHER is a week-long virtual hackathon focused on Women in Tech, Ethical AI, and Blockchain for social good. It's designed to create an inclusive space where participants build technology that empowers communities and promotes positive change.
Everyone is welcome! While we especially encourage women and non-binary individuals to participate, allies from all backgrounds are invited. Students from all universities, high school students (16+), and participants from all majors are welcome.
No prior experience required! We'll provide comprehensive resources, tutorials, and mentorship focused on ethical AI and blockchain for social good. Many successful projects come from teams learning these technologies during the hackathon.

Focus on projects that empower communities, promote inclusion, and create positive social impact. This could include solutions for women in tech mentorship, ethical AI applications, blockchain for transparency, or accessible technology for diverse communities.
