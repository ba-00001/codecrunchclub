Hack University Ecosystem

Get Involved with Hack University

Hack University brings together multiple student-led organizations to support learning, mentorship, and hands-on projects in technology and innovation:

  • Code Crunch Club
  • GDG @ FIU (Google Developer Group on Campus)
  • ColorStack @ FIU
  • CAHSI @ FIU Student Club
  • Raspberry Pi Code Crunch
1. Applicants – Speaker or E-Board Member

This section is for applicants who would like to become a guest speaker or join an e-board within the Hack University ecosystem (any of the organizations listed above).

How to apply:

Step 1: Complete the official Hack University Speaker / E-Board Application form.
Step 2: After you submit the application, send us a direct message on LinkedIn (Code Crunch Club). We use this DM to schedule a meeting, verify details, and match you with the most relevant organization.

DM us on LinkedIn
2. Support, Partnerships, or Contact – Hack University

If you are interested in supporting Hack University through funding, in-kind donations, collaborations, or partnerships, we would be happy to connect and explore how your contribution can impact workshops, hackathons, mentorship programs, and community events.

How to get in touch:

  • Option A – Support / Partnership Form: Complete the Support / Partnership Interest Form with your organization details, preferred level of support, or collaboration idea.
  • Option B – Direct Contact: Email us directly to coordinate donations, sponsorships, partnerships, or other ways to work together.
DM us on LinkedIn

Donor, sponsorship, and partnership inquiries can support one or more organizations in the Hack University ecosystem, including Code Crunch, GDG @ FIU, ColorStack @ FIU, CAHSI @ FIU, and Raspberry Pi Code Crunch.

3. Book a Virtual Coffee Chat

We also host informal 1:1 and small-group conversations for students and executive board members. To request a coffee chat, please DM us on LinkedIn (Code Crunch Club) and mention that you are interested in a Virtual Coffee Chat.

Virtual Coffee Chat

For:

  • Students
  • Executive Board
  • Executive Board Interviews
  • Casual Discussions

How to book: DM us on LinkedIn (Code Crunch Club) with “Virtual Coffee Chat” in your message and include a brief note about what you’d like to talk about. We’ll reply with available times to book your coffee chat.
