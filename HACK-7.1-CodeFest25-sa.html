General Build [Track #1 â€” Hackathon Competition]

Build a marketing, business, finance, or AI-powered tool that delivers real measurable value. This track focuses on prototypes that solve operational problems, automate workflows, provide analytics, or enhance decision-making. Think dashboards, AI agents, automation tools, or data-driven insights that teams could realistically pilot within 3â€“12 months.

  • Create an AI marketing assistant that automates customer insights, content generation, or engagement analytics.
  • Build a financial planning or budgeting dashboard using live data, forecasting models, or AI-supported recommendations.
  • Develop an internal business operations tool â€” workflow automation, task routing, KPI monitoring, or team productivity analytics.
  • Design a customer-intelligence or market-research platform powered by sentiment analysis, LLMs, or trend prediction.
  • Build a revenue analysis or pricing optimization system that helps businesses make data-driven decisions quickly.
  • Create AI agents that reason, plan, and act to automate repetitive business or administrative processes.
  • Develop a finance/fintech tool â€” spending trackers, fraud signals, credit analysis, or cash-flow forecasting models.

Venture Arena (Pitch) [Track #2 â€” Business Venture Arena Competition]

Design a no-code startup, program, or business venture that solves a real marketing, business, or finance problem using strategy, research, and AI-enabled insights. Clearly define your customer, validate your problem, build a value proposition, and outline a realistic business model along with a launch and growth plan.

  • Pitch a marketing-tech or AI-driven growth startup that helps brands acquire, engage, or retain customers more efficiently.
  • Design a business consulting venture that uses AI tools to help small businesses improve operations, finances, or branding.
  • Create a financial literacy or personal finance education platform targeted at students, families, or underserved communities.
  • Propose a marketplace or service connecting creators, freelancers, students, or niche communities to new opportunities.
  • Build a data-driven business idea solving gaps in customer research, consumer behavior, engagement analytics, or retention.
  • Pitch a subscription-based venture offering curated AI tools, business templates, workshops, or coaching programs.
  • Design a scalable startup leveraging partnerships with schools, nonprofits, brands, tech communities, or enterprises.

Deliverables: Pitch deck, short video, and a brief executive summary. Read the Hacker Guide for full detailed instructions and requirements.
