Hacking in Paradise

🎉 About 305 Tropical Hacks

Welcome to 305 Tropical Hacks, a premier hackathon in the heart of Miami! This event brings together students, developers, and tech enthusiasts to build **innovative projects**, solve real-world challenges, and network with **industry leaders**. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, there's something for everyone!

🌟 Why Participate?

  • 🚀 Build & Launch - Develop real-world projects in 24-48 hours.
  • 💡 Learn & Innovate - Gain hands-on experience with the latest tech tools.
  • 🤝 Network & Connect - Meet industry professionals, mentors, and like-minded peers.
  • 🏆 Compete for Prizes - Win amazing awards, swag, and recognition.
  • 🎓 Opportunities for Growth - Stand out to potential recruiters and unlock possibilities for internships and scholarships (not guaranteed but highly encouraged!).
  • 🌴 Enjoy Miami’s Vibes - Experience a fun, high-energy atmosphere.

📅 Event Details

  • 📍 Location: Florida International University, Miami, FL
  • 📅 Date: Spring & Fall 2025
  • ⏳ Duration: 24-48 hours of non-stop coding, networking, and fun!
  • 🎯 Eligibility: Open to students, developers, and tech enthusiasts.

Spring 2025 Tracks

🌍 Sustainability

Projects that leverage technology to drive sustainable solutions, reduce waste, or promote eco-friendly habits. Examples include carbon footprint tracking apps, smart waste management, and renewable energy tools.

🤖 AI & Machine Learning

Projects focused on creating smart solutions with AI to analyze data, enhance automation, and improve decision-making. This includes chatbots, predictive analytics, and deep learning applications.

💼 Fintech

Projects that transform the financial landscape by improving transactions, banking, and personal finance management. Examples include fraud detection, budgeting apps, and blockchain solutions.

🎮 Gaming

Projects that create immersive and interactive gaming experiences, ranging from mobile and console games to VR and AR applications.

🏥 Healthcare

Projects that improve healthcare solutions through digital tools, AI-driven diagnostics, or wellness tracking. This includes mental health support apps, remote patient monitoring, and telehealth services.

🎭 Entertainment

Projects that explore new ways to consume, create, and distribute media. Examples include AI-powered content creators, streaming innovations, and interactive storytelling.

📚 Education

Projects that enhance learning experiences through technology. This includes online education platforms, AI tutors, and interactive training tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a marathon software product creation event. At 305 Hacks, you can look forward to amazing guest speakers, helpful workshops, tons of skilled mentors, and, of course, fun games and great swag.
When is 305 Hacks?
This year, 305 Hacks will take place in-person from March 28th-30th, 2025. It’ll be 36 hours of hacking, events, speakers, social events, and more!
Who can attend?
Any undergraduate or graduate student at any university in the world!
Why should I come to 305 Hacks?
305 Hacks offers an incredible opportunity to learn, network, and collaborate with fellow hackers. Beyond the workshops and mentorship, you’ll enjoy fun events, prizes, and a memorable hacking experience.
Is 305 Hacks in-person?
305 Hacks operates in a hybrid format. The hackathon itself will be conducted hybrid format, while the career expo will be exclusively in-person, providing opportunities for networking and engagement.
Do I need a team to participate?
Teams are not required to sign up, and you can join solo. You’ll have opportunities to form teams at our Team Formation events or on the Discord server. Teams can have up to 4 members.
Are travel stipends available?
Unfortunately, 305 Hacks does not provide travel stipends. However, we encourage participants to explore funding options through their universities or local organizations.
Do I need to pay to participate in 305 Hacks?
No, participation in 305 Hacks is completely free! We aim to make this event accessible to everyone interested.
