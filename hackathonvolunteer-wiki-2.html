Form No. 2 ‚Äî Event Registration (Official Support)

For volunteers who want to support a specific hackathon or semester event as an official supporter.

  • Who: Volunteers, industry professionals, partners, alumni, and community leaders
  • Roles: Dual Role (Judge & Mentor), Speaker (when applicable), Operations (when applicable)

Two Registration Paths

  • 1) Event Registration Only (No Donation) ‚Äî Volunteer participation only. No donation required. No certificate issued.
  • 2) Event Registration + Donation (Certificate Eligible) ‚Äî Volunteer participation with the option to request a certificate. Donation proof required.

Donation disclosure: Donations support the Hack University / Code Crunch organization as a whole and are not tied to a single event. Selected events are used only for tracking volunteer participation.

Verification & certificates: Maria verifies donation submissions only. After verification, our organization generates volunteer certificates and confirms participation, role completion, and eligibility. After donating, email Maria your confirmation and CC codecrunchclub@gmail.com. Additional instructions are included in Form No. 2.
