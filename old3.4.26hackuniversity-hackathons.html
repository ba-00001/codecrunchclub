Events & Hackathons

A hackathon is a fast-paced, hands-on problem-solving competition where you turn ideas into real solutions. Hack University hackathons bring students from all majors together to build, pitch, and compete across three different competition formats.

A hybrid global 3-in-1 competition that brings together technical innovation, entrepreneurship, and data analytics.

Technical Competition

Are you a CS or engineering major?

Tech Build Hackathon (Code or Hardware)

This competition is built for students who want to design, code, prototype, and ship technical solutions using software, hardware, or both together.

It is a strong fit for computer science, computer engineering, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and hands-on builders who want portfolio-ready project experience.

Start-Up Competition

Are you a business major or entrepreneur student?

Code to Cash: Develop the Next Business Startup Idea

This competition focuses on turning technical ideas into startup-ready concepts, market opportunities, and business pitches that can actually be explained to judges, users, and investors.

It is ideal for business, finance, marketing, management, entrepreneurship, and students who want to bridge innovation with commercial execution.

Analytics Competition

Are you a student interested in analytics or data science?

305 Business Analytics / Data Science

This competition is centered on data storytelling, analytics, dashboards, spreadsheet modeling, and decision-making under time pressure.

It works well for students exploring business analytics, information systems, statistics, applied math, operations, data science, or any role where insight and evidence drive strategy.

Open to everyone worldwide - beginners, advanced builders, and non-tech majors welcome. No prior experience needed. Ages 16+ accepted (under 18 requires parental or guardian consent).

Use the calendar below as the source of truth for all event details. The Zoom link on this page is the shared Zoom meeting used for all hybrid and virtual events. Please check and subscribe to the Hack University Google Calendar below to confirm that an event is scheduled before joining.
