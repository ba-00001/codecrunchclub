About Google Developer Group on Campus FIU

Google Developer Group on Campus FIU is the local chapter of Google Developer Groups, bringing together students passionate about Google technologies and software development. We provide a platform for learning, sharing knowledge, and connecting with the broader developer community.

To learn more about the GDG program, visit the official Google Developer Groups page.

Community

Connect with fellow developers, share ideas, and collaborate on projects. Our community welcomes developers of all skill levels.

Learning

Participate in workshops, tech talks, and hands-on sessions covering the latest Google technologies and development practices.

Development

Work on real projects using Google Cloud, Android, Web technologies, and other Google developer tools and platforms.

Networking

Build professional connections with industry experts, Google employees, and fellow developers in the global GDG network.
