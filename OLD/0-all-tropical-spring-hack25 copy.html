Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a marathon software product creation event. At Tropical Hacks, you can look forward to amazing guest speakers, helpful workshops, tons of skilled mentors, and, of course, fun games and great swag.
When is Tropical Hacks?
This year, Tropical Hacks will take place in-person from March 28th-30th, 2025. It’ll be 36 hours of hacking, events, speakers, social events, and more!
Who can attend?
Any undergraduate or graduate student at any university in the world!
Why should I come to Tropical Hacks?
Tropical Hacks offers an incredible opportunity to learn, network, and collaborate with fellow hackers. Beyond the workshops and mentorship, you’ll enjoy fun events, prizes, and a memorable hacking experience.
Is Tropical Hacks in-person?
Tropical Hacks operates in a hybrid format. The hackathon itself will be conducted virtually, while the career expo will be exclusively in-person, providing opportunities for networking and engagement.
Do I need a team to participate?
Teams are not required to sign up, and you can join solo. You’ll have opportunities to form teams at our Team Formation events or on the Discord server. Teams can have up to 4 members.
Are travel stipends available?
Unfortunately, Tropical Hacks does not provide travel stipends. However, we encourage participants to explore funding options through their universities or local organizations.
Do I need to pay to participate in Tropical Hacks?
No, participation in Tropical Hacks is completely free! We aim to make this event accessible to everyone interested.
