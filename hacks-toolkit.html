Our Partners

Our hackathons are made possible by collaboration with these amazing organizations. Connect with them to learn more about their initiatives and opportunities.

Code Crunch @ FIU

Open to all majors, universities, and students, Code Crunch is an interdisciplinary tech community focused on making tech education approachable for everyone. We provide hands-on coding workshops, technical interview prep, and collaborative projects that help students from any background build skills, grow professionally, and break into tech.

ColorStack @ FIU

Open to all majors, universities, and students, ColorStack at FIU supports those pursuing computer science by offering mentorship, community, and career development. While open to all, we focus on uplifting underserved students in CS by helping them navigate academic and career pathways in the tech industry with confidence and connection.

CAHSI @ FIU

Open to all majors, universities, and students, CAHSI (Computing Alliance of Hispanic-Serving Institutions) at FIU focuses on expanding access to computing research and graduate-level opportunities. Our chapter supports students through research experiences, academic mentoring, and guidance into advanced tech careers across industry and academia.
