🎉 Hackathon Volunteer Process

🔐 Access is granted only to the Gmail address you applied with.
If you're using a different Google account, please switch to the one used on your application form.
  1. Step 1: Fill out the " volunteer interest form."
  2. Step 2: If accepted, you will receive the "HACKATHON Volunteer Confirmation & Discord Form (Required) – Judges, Mentors & Workshop Leads."
    If your availability changes, simply fill out the form again. We will consider your most recent entry.
  3. Step 3: After submitting the second form:
    • Join the Discord server and introduce yourself!
    • Send a DM to @codecrunchclub to gain access to the private #hack-volunteers channel
    • Discord roles @hackathon-judge, @hackathon-mentor, and @hackathon-speaker with access to channel #hack-volunteers.
    • Check the #hack-volunteers channel for updates and announcements.
    • Only those who join Discord will be considered

In addition: If you're a Workshop Host, please complete the Hackathon Workshop Host/Speaker Interest Form.

⏳ Please be patient. Responses are reviewed every 5 business days.
⚡ The fastest way to receive a response after being accepted is to DM @codecrunchclub on Discord.
