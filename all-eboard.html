About the Job

Company Description:

Founded in August 2024, Code Crunch is a student-led club at Florida International University (FIU) dedicated to fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment for students passionate about coding and technology. The club’s mission is to enhance coding skills, promote innovation, and build a strong, diverse tech community at FIU.

With the launch of Code Crunch Global in October 2024, students worldwide can now join virtually to improve their technical skills and prepare for interviews in a supportive, community-driven environment.

Who Can Apply?

  • FIU students from all majors.
  • Students from other universities worldwide.
  • Industry professionals passionate about fostering innovation and supporting the next generation of tech talent.

Key Responsibilities

Industry Engagement:

  • Build connections with industry professionals to recruit guest speakers, panelists, and mentors.
  • Facilitate discussions and workshops on career preparation, industry trends, and technical innovation.

Event Coordination:

  • Plan and execute events such as workshops, hackathons, and panel discussions.
  • Manage event logistics, including scheduling, locations, materials, and virtual setups.

Grant and Funding Support:

  • Research and identify grant opportunities to support club initiatives.
  • Assist in writing and submitting grant proposals.
  • Collaborate on building partnerships with organizations for sponsorship and funding.

Qualifications

General Requirements:

  • A passion for technology, education, and fostering diversity and inclusion.
  • Strong communication and organizational skills.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team setting.
  • Enthusiasm to contribute to the growth of a student-led community.

Preferred (But Not Required):

  • Experience in event planning, grant writing, or engaging with industry professionals.
  • Familiarity with project management tools (e.g., Trello, Asana) or fundraising platforms.
  • Knowledge of coding and technology concepts.

No prior experience is required; we welcome individuals eager to learn and make an impact.
