Join the Code Crunch Executive-Board ✨

Be part of the leadership team that drives innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity at Code Crunch!

Thank you for your interest in applying for the E-Board!

If you have any questions about the application, feel free to email us. Don't forget to check out the Position Descriptions!

View Position Descriptions

How This Application Works

In the first section of this application, you will be asked: "Which position are you interested in applying for?" You'll then choose from the available Shadowing E-Board positions and respond to specific questions related to each one.

You may apply for multiple positions—just make sure to answer the corresponding questions for each shadowing role you're interested in so you can be considered for all selected positions.

Time Commitment: 5 hours per week

Who Can Apply?

  • FIU students from all majors
  • Students from other universities worldwide
  • Industry professionals passionate about fostering innovation

General Requirements

  • A passion for technology, education, and fostering diversity and inclusion
  • Strong communication and organizational skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team setting
  • Enthusiasm to contribute to the growth of a student-led community

Preferred (But Not Required)

  • Experience in event planning, public speaking, or community outreach
  • Familiarity with project management tools or technical platforms
  • Knowledge of coding and technology concepts

No prior experience is required; we welcome individuals eager to learn and make an impact.

✨ What You'll Gain by Joining ✨

  • Leadership Experience: Develop critical leadership skills managing dynamic projects
  • Professional Development: Bolster your resume with real-world experience
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with industry professionals and peers
  • Exclusive Perks: Access special events, workshops, and mentorship programs
  • Impact & Legacy: Help build a diverse and innovative tech community
  • Personal Growth: Enhance communication and problem-solving skills
