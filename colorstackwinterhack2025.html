Hacker Guide

Overview

Have free time during break and wondering how to spend it? We got you. ColorStack is hosting a Winter Break Hackathon! Build and ship a project to production with other ColorStack members.

When

The hackathon will be open from Friday, Dec 19 to Monday, Jan 12.

  • • Fri, Dec 19: Hackathon officially starts for Team Formation & Networking
  • • Mon, Dec 15: Opening Ceremony (Theme + Awards Reveal) @ 3:00 PM EST
  • • Mon, Jan 12: Submissions due
  • • Live Expo — tentative on Jan 15
  • • Fri, Jan 16: Finalists announced
  • • Fri, Jan 23: Finalists present at the Spring Career Fair

Theme

The hackathon theme is hidden and will be unveiled live at the Opening Ceremony on Dec 15 @ 3:00 PM EST.

Theme: Responsible AI

This year’s theme focuses on building ethical, inclusive, and human-centered AI systems.

Theme Resources

  • • Kapor Foundation — Responsible AI
  • • Humanity AI

Teams

Teams will consist of 1–4 ColorStack members. Solo projects are allowed, but collaboration is encouraged!

Project Requirements

Your project must be publicly accessible. Example: If you build a web app, we should be able to open the live URL.

Submissions

All submissions are due on Friday, Jan 12 @ 11:59 PM PT.

Prizes

Finalists will be selected in the following categories:

  • • Best Overall
  • • Most Commercially Viable
  • • People’s Choice
  • • Best Underclassmen (100% underclassmen team)

Category awards will be unveiled at the Opening Ceremony (Mon, Dec 15 @ 3:00 PM EST).

Each finalist receives a $100 gift card of their choice (ColorStack Merch, DoorDash, etc.).

Presentations

The lead from each winning team will present at the Spring Career Fair on Friday, Jan 23 (time TBD).

Beyond the Hackathon

Teams that make their project open source and continue development will have their hosting & infrastructure costs covered by ColorStack for a full year.
